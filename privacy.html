Privacy Policy

Last updated Jan 16, 2026

Simple Pace ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Running pace, distance, time, and preferences
  • Settings: Your preferences for the app's functionality and appearance

Information We Don't Collect

  • We do not collect personal contact information (email, phone numbers, addresses)
  • We do not access your device's contacts, photos, or other personal data
  • We do not track your location or use analytics services
  • We do not collect any information about you personally

How We Use Information

The information you enter is used solely to calculate running pace, time, and distance.

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties
  • Your information never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it

iCloud Sync (Optional)

  • If you enable iCloud, your data may sync across your Apple devices
  • This uses Apple's secure iCloud infrastructure
  • We do not have access to your iCloud data
  • You can disable iCloud sync in your device settings

Data Backup

  • The app creates local backups on your device for data recovery
  • These backups remain on your device only
  • No backups are sent to external services

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties. Your information remains private to you.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please email us at hello@simplepacecalc.com.
